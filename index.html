My strengths:

I also am very good at painting in acrylic. I feel that at the start of everyone's art journey, whether or not they go on this journey, they are taught all the art mediums known. Another example would be how I practiced in all the mediums, but even though I did well, I found that I could not really enjoy doing the other mediums as much as I did in acrylic.(Hopefully, I will enjoy oil paint as well, haha-)
